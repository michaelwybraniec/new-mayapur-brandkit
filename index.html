Brand Kit

Welcome to a NEW MAYAPUR Brand Kit!

Introduction

The NEW MAYAPUR Brand Kit is designed to provide a consistent visual identity for the NEW MAYAPUR brand, emphasizing rich colors, elegant typography, and thoughtfully chosen design elements. This guide serves as a reference for maintaining brand integrity across all digital and print materials. The color palette includes primary, secondary, neutral, and background colors that represent the spirit and aesthetic of NEW MAYAPUR, blending bold and soft tones to convey both tradition and modernity. Each color is carefully selected to evoke a sense of elegance, warmth, and professionalism, while also providing flexibility for various design contexts.

In addition to colors, the typography reflects the essence of NEW MAYAPUR, using the Yeseva One font for titles and headings to create a bold and distinguished presence, paired with the Montserrat font for body text to ensure clarity and readability. This brand kit also features button styles, light and dark section examples, and consistent spacing and alignment principles to enhance user experience and maintain aesthetic harmony. Whether you are creating web designs, marketing materials, or internal documents, this guide will help you apply the key visual elements that define the NEW MAYAPUR brand.
